Copy editing

(8 weeks)

 

MS Word

{manuscript)

Primary goals of editing:

       enhance clarity

       achieve standardisation

       improve expression

(Needs vary from project to project. Copy editors may also be called upon to check facts, spot reasoning/logic errors etc.)

Copy editor with WT

2

Typesetting

(aka Pagesetting/ Layouting)

(3 weeks)

 

Adobe Creative Suite (e.g., InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop)

Convert edited manuscript into codex format:

       Lay out manuscript and apply formatting, pagination and suitable design elements

       Prepare copyright page bearing the ISBN no.

       Set the book cover as a spread (front, back, spine), with ISBN barcode at back

       Assemble all of the above into proof

 

WT with design house

3

Proofreading

(4 weeks to do two rounds)

 

Adobe PDF

(proof)

Check for errors in formatting, typos etc.

(Avoid major changes such as shifting, rephrasing and addition/removal of parts which should be done at Stage 1.)

 

WT with design house

4

Pre-press

(1 week)

 

High resolution Adobe PDF

Liaise with printer; ensure final file conforms to printing requirements

Design house to facilitate

5

Computer-to-Plate (CTP) Offset Lithography

Print and deliver books to WT/intended location
